Lore

Anna came from the land of cold winters and warm summers, from a town that stands on a mighty river Volga and Zhiguli Mountains. Samara, Russia. Her mother was a tourist and father was a programmer. How could she in these circumstances wave away a desire to explore something new every day? Each holidays and weekends, she tried to get outside, and in high school, Anna started to code the languages that she studied. Eventually, she decided to become a mathematic. However, getting a math degree was not enough and she started to learn what she was most interested in. Interface development. It's what you can draw something you see in your favorite language — the language of mathematic. She started to learn HTML and CSS, worked in this sphere almost 3 years, created a lot for herself, obstinately evolved.

One cold winter day, at the peak of the cold in her town, she came home after a graduate, desolated, turned on a computer. That's it. She's a computer safety specialist now. What's next? What purpose she's got now? She needed to play that moment. But play what? All the games were not so interesting, it seemed, she played all of them. Anna asked her friends. 'League of Legends, of course!' they answered. She couldn't play anything else since. This game seemed perfect, you've got everything in it — tactics, skills, sapid champions' background. She tried to play with her high skill friends to learn everything, to become the best.

Work started to bring less pleasure as before. There was a need to do your favorite thing in a place, where you're truly in need, with people, who share her interests. And then she decided to join League of Legends.
